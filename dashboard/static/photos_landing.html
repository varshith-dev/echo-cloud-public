Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about OQENS Photos.

Does OQENS Photos compress my images?
No. OQENS Photos stores your images in their original, uncompressed resolution to ensure zero quality loss. We believe in pixel-perfect preservation.
Can I share albums publicly?
Yes, you can generate secure public links for any album. You can also add password protection and expiration dates to these links for client proofing.
Is my EXIF metadata preserved?
Absolutely. Your camera settings (aperture, shutter speed, ISO), location data, and original timestamps are kept fully intact and untouched.
Are RAW files supported?
Yes, we support uploading and storing all major RAW formats alongside standard JPEG, PNG, HEIC, and WebP files.
Who owns the rights to my photos?
You do. You retain 100% copyright ownership of your images. We do not claim any rights to your work, nor do we use them to train AI models.
